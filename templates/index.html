About the App

This app is a text summarizer that uses the TextRank algorithm to summarize text. It is a simple extractive summarization technique that is based on the PageRank algorithm used by Google Search.

It works by first splitting the text into sentences and then splitting the sentences into words. It then computes a score for every word in the text. The score for a word is calculated by adding up the scores of the words that co-occur with it in the text. The score for a sentence is calculated by adding up the scores of the words that make up the sentence. The summary is then generated by including the sentences with the highest scores.
